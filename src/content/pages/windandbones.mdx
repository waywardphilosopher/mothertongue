Dr. Hannah Stevens and Dr. Will Buckingham are co-directors of Wind&Bones CIC.

We run projects that use writing, storytelling and philosophy as catalysts for positive social change.

Registered as a social enterprise in Dundee, Scotland, we work across the globe. We currently divide our time between Dundee and Tainan, Taiwan.
